Introduction

Global Impact is a business that is going to help all around the world giving teens and young adults the confidence to express themselves through different clothing. Expressing yourself through fashion allows you to make a statement about who you are as a person without being judged or ridiculed by others. Furthermore, it can often be easier for people to appear more confident if they aren't afraid of what other people will think of them when it comes to their style.
